Cost model

1
Each recipe action node currently generates a custom AI icon at $0.04 per call. With 82.7% unique descriptions, the cache rarely helps.
2
Text embeddings of 2,000 unique action descriptions were clustered into archetypes using K-means + UMAP. Each archetype covers a coherent visual theme.
3
For free-tier users, the nearest archetype icon is served instead of generating a new one — zero marginal cost per node.
4
At ~15 action nodes per recipe and the measured coverage rate, the savings compound quickly across thousands of free-tier recipes per month.
